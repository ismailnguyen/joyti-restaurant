Our story

Our Joyti Restaurant is very close to major tourist attractions like Montparnasse Tower, Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum. The restaurant is right on Main Street where parking for tourist buses is easy. Our beautiful restaurant is having 75+ seats to provide quick table service (not the buffet) to the customers so they can continue their meals without waiting.

​

As we are in service since last 20 years, we understand tourist group’s & executive group’s needs very well when it comes to Indian cuisine.

We provide lunch, dinner service with drinks in restaurant as per your requirements. We also provide take away service. We can prepare menu for groups as their requirements like veg & non-veg dishes.

We welcome our clients to come & enjoy our delicious Indian cuisine. Europe is full of tourist attraction but still an Indian tourist likes a tasty, spicy Indian food in homely environment & we make sure they get it all here in Paris. We feel great when we see a smile on customer’s faces & that’s our note of appreciation from them. We would like to keep providing best service to our customers to make their lunch and dinner as our best. As our customer’s satisfaction is our satisfaction, we can assure them the best service at Joyti Restaurant, Paris. For any information & queries, you can always contact us.
